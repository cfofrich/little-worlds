Little World: Stickers Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 15, 2026

Little World: Stickers is designed for local, private play. We do not run ads, do not require accounts, and do not sell personal data.

Information We Collect

  • We do not collect personal information from children or parents.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking SDKs, or third-party ad networks.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, photos, camera data, microphone data, or device identifiers for profiling.

How the App Works

  • Gameplay and settings are handled on-device.
  • No account sign-in is required.
  • No cloud backend is required for core play.

Feedback Email

The app includes a “Send Feedback” option that opens your own email app. If you choose to email us, we receive only the information you include in that email.

Children’s Privacy

Little World: Stickers is intended for young children and families. We intentionally avoid data collection and behavioral tracking.

Future Changes

If we add diagnostics, analytics, or any new data collection in future versions, we will update this policy before release and provide any disclosures/consents required by law or platform rules.
